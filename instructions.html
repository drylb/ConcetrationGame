Click the "Start" button to begin the game. The cards will be shuffled and arranged facedown on the screen.

Click on any two cards to flip them over and reveal their face. If the two cards match, they will stay face up, and you will earn a point. If the cards don't match, they will flip back facedown. Remember the positions of the cards to make successful matches.

Your goal is to find and match all the pairs of cards on the board. Try to complete the game with the fewest number of moves possible.

Once all the pairs have been matched, a message will appear congratulating you on completing the game. Your score and the number of moves taken will be displayed.
